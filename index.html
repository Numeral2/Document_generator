How to Use

Follow these steps to use the bot:

  1. Click "Use Bot" and log in or sign up if it's your first time.
  2. Upload your .docx file by clicking "Choose File".
  3. The content of the file will be displayed in the text area.
  4. The textarea can be expanded by holding the bottom-right corner and dragging it.
  5. Add placeholders like (Client_name) and replacement text. Placeholders must be in brackets in textarea, but not necessary in placeholders tab.
  6. Click "Generate Text" to replace placeholders with your text.
